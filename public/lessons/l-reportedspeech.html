Reported Speech

เรียนรู้การถ่ายทอดคำพูดด้วยการเปลี่ยนรูปประโยค

🔸 กฎ: เปลี่ยน Tense → ย้อนหลัง

  • Present → Past
  • Past Simple → Past Perfect
  • Will → Would
  • Can → Could

🔸 โครงสร้าง

โครงสร้าง: [Subject] + said (that) + [reported sentence]

🔸 ตัวอย่าง

  • “I am tired,” she said.
    → She said (that) she was tired.
  • “We watched a movie,” they said.
    → They said they had watched a movie.
  • “I will help you,” he told me.
    → He told me that he would help me.
